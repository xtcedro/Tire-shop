Refund Policy

Understand our refund policy for tire products and services.

Refund Eligibility

Refunds are available for new tires returned within 14 days of purchase with proof of receipt and provided they are unused and undamaged.

Non-Refundable Items

Used tires, special orders, and services rendered (e.g., installation, repairs) are non-refundable.

Refund Process

Approved refunds will be processed within 7 business days to the original payment method.
