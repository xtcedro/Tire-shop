About Us

Our Story

Welcome to Tito's Tire Shop! We have proudly served the Oklahoma City community for many years, offering high-quality tire services and exceptional customer care. Whether you need new or used tires, installations, repairs, or brake services, our team is here to help.

Our Mission

At Tito's Tire Shop, our mission is to provide fast, affordable, and reliable tire services to keep you and your family safe on the road. We believe in honesty, quality, and excellent service.

Why Choose Us?

  • Wide selection of new and used tires.
  • Affordable pricing for tire repairs and services.
  • Bilingual service in English and Spanish.
  • Friendly and knowledgeable staff.
