About Tito's Tire Shop

Your Trusted Tire Shop in Oklahoma City

Our Mission

At Tito's Tire Shop, our mission is to provide reliable and affordable tire services to the Oklahoma City community. From tire installations and repairs to wheel balancing and brake services, we are committed to keeping you safe on the road with high-quality solutions and excellent customer service.

Our Vision

We envision becoming the go-to tire shop in Oklahoma City, known for our expertise, affordability, and innovative features like Tito's TreadMatch OKC. By prioritizing customer satisfaction and safety, we aim to build lasting relationships with our clients and the community.
